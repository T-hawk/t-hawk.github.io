Play game

I challenged myself to make a game within a weekend. By the end of the weekend, I had failed the challenge, but I eventually finished the project. The theme I gave myself was radiation. In this game, solve puzzles and face challenges, but be careful! Some materials are radioactive and cause you to lose health.
