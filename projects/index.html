Discord Button

A discord bot created to have fun with my friends. The idea is that the button is constantly moving closer to death, and the only way to revive the button is to press it. When the button is pressed, the presser is assigned a rank, and the closer the button is to death, the higher rank the presser is assigned.

July 2020

Picka Movie

During the beginning of the pandemic, my family and I spent a lot of time watching movies together. This resulted in some disagreement on what movie to watch. I thought of a simple solution to this problem: democracy. I created an app that allowed you to create a voting session, share the link with your family, and decide a movie within half an hour. Unfortunetly, I never posted all my code to github and the working code was deleted when I got rid of my old computer.

March 2020

Wheel of Misfortune

My 9th grade geometry teacher liked to play this review game called Wheel of Misfortune. The idea of the game was that the first team to answer a question correctly got to spin a wheel that would either reward them or punish them. I recreated the game online using rails as a backend and Svelte on the frontend.
